## faCount (UCSC)

Outputs general nucleotide stats for each sequence in fasta file.       

[Click here for GitHub](https://github.com/adamlabadorf/ucsc_tools/blob/master/executables/faCount)

```
#run as
faCount myfasta.fa
```
